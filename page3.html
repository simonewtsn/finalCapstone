Shipping Container Homes

What is a Shipping Container Home?

A shipping container home is a type of home, usually a small or medium-sized dwelling, that is made out of one or more shipping containers. This type of home has become increasingly popular in recent years as an alternative to traditional construction and due to the availability of inexpensive, used shipping containers.

Pro's Con's
Affordability: Shipping containers are relatively inexpensive compared to traditional construction materials.
Durability: Shipping containers are designed to be extremely durable. They are watertight and can withstand harsh weather conditions.
Flexibility: Shipping containers can easily be adapted to fit different types of needs, from a single-family home to an office or retail space.
 Noise: Shipping containers are made of metal, which can create a lot of noise when exposed to wind or rain.
Insulation: Shipping containers are not insulated, so additional insulation will need to be added.
Building Regulations: Building regulations may vary from one location to another and shipping container homes may not meet all requirements.
