Hobbit Homes (Earth-Sheltered)

What is a Earth Sheltered Home?

A hobbit home is an earth sheltered home that is designed to blend in with the natural environment. The home is typically made of natural materials such as stone, wood, and earth and is often built into the surrounding landscape.

Pro's Con's
Energy efficiency: Earth sheltered homes require less energy to heat and cool than traditional homes, as the soil temperature remains relatively constant and provides insulation.
Durability: The materials used in earth sheltered homes are usually long-lasting and require little maintenance.
Aesthetics: Hobbit homes can provide a unique and attractive look to any landscape.
 Cost: Building an earth sheltered home is usually more expensive than a traditional home, as it requires specialized expertise and materials.
Moisture: Due to the close proximity of the home to the ground, moisture can become an issue. Proper drainage and ventilation must be taken into account when constructing an earth sheltered home.
