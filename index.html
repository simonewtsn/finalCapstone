Alternative Homes

"Alternative housing is about enabling people to live in a way that is affordable, reasonable, and environmentally-friendly. It is about finding solutions that offer a better quality of life and reduce our environmental footprint." - Alexi Marmot, Founder of the Sustainable Homes Initiative

Welcome to the blog that explores alternative homes and ways of living that can help us lead more meaningful and fulfilling lives. There are a variety of interesting homes and ideas. This blog looks at the benefits and challenges associated with these alternative lifestyles, and how to make them work for you. Providing practical advice on how to make the transition to alternative living.

Alternative homes are becoming increasingly popular as people look for ways to reduce their environmental impact and live in a more sustainable way. From tiny homes to off-grid cabins, there are many interesting and unique alternatives to traditional homes.

Here are some examples of the types of alternative homes:
  • Tiny homes
  • Shipping container homes
  • Yurts
  • Earthships
  • Converted buses
  • Teardrop trailers
  • Converted vans
  • Cabins
  • Sheds
  • Igloos
